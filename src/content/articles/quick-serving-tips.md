## Serve It Right

1. **Chill properly** - 38-45Â°F for most styles
2. **Use a glass** - Releases aromas trapped in the can
3. **Pour with vigor** - A finger of foam is good
4. **Clean glasses** - Soap residue kills foam
5. **Drink fresh** - Check packaging dates

That's it. Enjoy your beer.
