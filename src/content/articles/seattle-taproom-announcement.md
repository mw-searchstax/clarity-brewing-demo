## We're Coming to Seattle!

Clarity Brewing is thrilled to announce our third taproom location in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood, opening Summer 2025.

## Location

**Address:** 1420 12th Avenue, Seattle, WA 98122

The 3,500 square foot space occupies a historic building in the heart of Capitol Hill, one of Seattle's most vibrant neighborhoods.

## What to Expect

- Full lineup of Clarity Brewing beers on tap
- Seattle-exclusive rotating taps
- Local food truck partnerships
- Indoor/outdoor seating for 75
- Weekly brewery tours

## Grand Opening

We're planning a major grand opening celebration in July 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be first to know the exact date.

## Why Seattle?

The Pacific Northwest has embraced non-alcoholic beer like few other regions. Seattle's health-conscious, craft-beer-loving culture makes it a perfect fit for Clarity Brewing.
