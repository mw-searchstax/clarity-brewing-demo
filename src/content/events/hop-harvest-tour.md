## What Happened

In September 2024, we took 20 guests to visit Hill Country Hops, our partner hop farm just outside Fredericksburg, Texas.

## The Day Included

- Bus transportation from Austin Taproom
- Farm tour during active harvest
- Hands-on hop picking experience
- Fresh-hop beer tasting (brewed the day before with just-picked hops)
- Farm-to-table lunch
- Take-home bag of fresh hops
