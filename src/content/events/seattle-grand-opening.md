## Seattle, We're Here!

After months of anticipation, Clarity Brewing officially opens our Seattle taproom. Join us for the biggest party we've ever thrown.

## Free All-Day Celebration

No tickets required - just show up and celebrate!

**What's Free:**
- Admission all day
- Live music (three bands)
- First 100 guests get a free pint glass
- Raffle entries for everyone

**Available for Purchase:**
- All Clarity Brewing beers
- Seattle-exclusive releases
- Food from local trucks
- Merchandise

## Seattle-Exclusive Releases

We're debuting three beers you can ONLY get at the Seattle taproom:

1. **Rainier Fog IPA** - Hazy IPA with Washington-grown hops
2. **Pike Place Porter** - Coffee porter with beans from the original Starbucks
3. **Puget Pils** - Crisp pilsner for Pacific Northwest summers
