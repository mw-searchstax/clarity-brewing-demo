## Celebrate Spring with Clarity Brewing

Our annual Spring Tasting Festival is back and bigger than ever! This all-day celebration brings together craft beer lovers, curious newcomers, and everyone who appreciates great beverages.

## What's Included

Your $35 ticket gets you:

- **Unlimited tastings** of all Clarity Brewing beers (current lineup + exclusive previews)
- **Commemorative tasting glass** to take home
- **Food pairings** curated by local restaurants
- **Live music** from Austin's best local bands
- **Brewery tours** every hour on the hour
- **Meet the brewers** - Jake and Priya will be there all day!

## Special Releases

We're debuting **three new beers** at this year's festival:

1. **Spring Fling Saison** - Light, peppery, perfect for warm weather
2. **Citrus Burst Radler** - Our lager meets fresh grapefruit
3. **Mystery Beer #3** - You'll have to come to find out!

Festival attendees get first access to purchase these limited releases.
