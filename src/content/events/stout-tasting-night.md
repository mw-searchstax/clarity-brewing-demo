## A Night for Dark Beer Lovers

Join us for an evening celebrating the rich, complex world of stouts and porters.

## The Lineup

We'll taste five dark beers in progression from light to intense:

1. **Dark Roast Porter** - Our coffee-infused flagship porter
2. **Midnight Stout** - Classic dry stout, clean and roasty
3. **Breakfast Stout** - Decadent oatmeal stout with maple
4. **Guest Beer** - Rotating selection from a partner brewery
5. **Barrel-Aged Preview** - A sneak peek at an upcoming release

## Chocolate Pairings

Each beer is paired with artisan chocolates from Denver's own Ritual Chocolate:
- 70% dark with porter
- Espresso chocolate with stout
- Salted caramel with breakfast stout
- And more...

## Learn from the Experts

Our brewing team will guide you through:
- The difference between stout and porter
- Roasted malt varieties and their flavors
- How we preserve chocolate/coffee notes in NA beer
- Food pairing principles for dark beers

- **When:** Saturday, March 15, 7:00 PM
- **Duration:** 90 minutes

- **Capacity:** 40 guests
