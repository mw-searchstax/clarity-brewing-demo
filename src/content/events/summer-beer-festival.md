## The Biggest NA Beer Festival in Texas

For three days in June, Austin becomes the capital of non-alcoholic craft beer. Join us for Summer Beer Festival 2025, featuring over 50 NA beers from 20+ breweries.

## Three Days of Beer

### Friday, June 20
**Opening Night Party**
- 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- 25 beers available
- Live DJ
- Food trucks open

### Saturday, June 21
**Main Festival Day**
- 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- All 50+ beers available
- Three live bands
- Brewing demonstrations
- Full food truck lineup

### Sunday, June 22
**Farewell Brunch**
- 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Brunch-friendly beers featured
- Bloody Mary bar (NA, of course)
- Acoustic music
- Relaxed vibe
