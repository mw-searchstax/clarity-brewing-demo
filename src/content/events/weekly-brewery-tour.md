## Every Saturday at 2 PM

Join us every Saturday afternoon for an exclusive look behind the scenes at Clarity Brewing's Austin facility.

## What You'll See

Our 60-minute tour covers the entire brewing process:

- **Grain Room** - See (and smell!) the malts we use
- **Brewhouse** - Watch our 15-barrel system in action
- **Fermentation** - Learn how yeast creates flavor
- **Dealcoholization** - Our proprietary low-temperature vacuum process
- **Packaging** - Where beer meets can

## Tastings Included

Your tour includes samples of:
- 4 beers from our core lineup
- 1 tank sample (beer not yet released!)
- Pilot batch preview when available

## Meet the Team

Our brewing staff leads each tour. Ask questions - they love talking about beer.

## Details

- **When:** Every Saturday, 2:00 PM
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Price:** $15 (includes tastings and souvenir glass)
- **Age:** 21+ only (we still check IDs for our own purposes)
- **Capacity:** 25 people max

## What to Wear

Closed-toe shoes required in the brewery. The facility is temperature-controlled but dress comfortably.

## Reservations

Book online or call the taproom. Walk-ins accepted if space available, but reservations recommended.

*Tours run rain or shine - we're indoors!*
