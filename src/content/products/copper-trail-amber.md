## About Copper Trail Amber

Copper Trail is our crowd-pleaser - an amber ale that bridges the gap between light lagers and bold craft styles. The beautiful copper color hints at the rich malt flavors within.

### Tasting Notes

- **Aroma:** Warm caramel and toasted bread
- **Taste:** Sweet malt upfront, balanced by gentle hop bitterness
- **Finish:** Clean with lingering toffee notes

### The Malt Story

We use a complex grain bill to achieve Copper Trail's signature flavor:
- **Munich Malt** - Provides the rich amber color
- **Crystal Malt** - Adds caramel sweetness
- **Victory Malt** - Contributes toasty, biscuit notes
